DocDash is a cloud-based web software product only for doctors/Clinics so that they can provide efﬁcient care and an excellent experience to their patients.

Soon on your AppStores

The Mobile Application software  HeyDoc empowers patients to ﬁnd the right doctors and instantly book the appointments.  P atients can search on HeyDoc for doctors online based on medical specialty, locality, symptoms,names of doctors and the clinics/hospitals.  The Electronic medical records (EMR) replace the old school paper based medical reports and prescriptions , thereby saving paper ,hence more financially efficient service . A user(patient) can share these medical records with other doctors for a 2nd opinion by sharing unique report urls.
